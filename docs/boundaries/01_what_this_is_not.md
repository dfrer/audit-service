# What This Service Is and Is Not

## What This Service IS

### A Practical Audit
We look at how you actually use AI -- your tools, workflows, setups, and processes -- and tell you what works, what is wasting money, what is risky, and what to fix first.

### Fixed Price, Fixed Scope
You know the cost and what you get before you buy. No hourly billing. No scope creep. No surprise invoices.

### Fast Turnaround
48 hours for the basic audit. 5 days max with implementation scaffolding.

### Honest Findings
We will tell you when a tool is wasting money, when your local setup is a maintenance nightmare, when your "automated" workflow still requires manual work at every step.

### Actionable Plans
Every audit produces a prioritized action plan with severity ratings, effort estimates, and concrete steps.

## What This Service Is NOT

### Deep Technical Consulting
We do not embed with your team for weeks. We do not architect your entire AI infrastructure from scratch. We audit what you have and give you a plan.

### Custom Software Development
We do not build custom AI applications, train models, write production codebases, or develop ML pipelines. The Scaffold tier includes one PR or setup guide, not a full build.

### Legal or Compliance Advisory
We can flag privacy concerns and data handling risks, but we are not a law firm. For compliance questions (GDPR, HIPAA, SOC 2), hire a compliance specialist.

### IT Managed Services
We do not manage your servers, run your helpdesk, or maintain your systems ongoing. We audit your AI usage and give recommendations.

### AI Training or Education
We do not teach AI literacy, run workshops, or create training programs. We audit your specific setup and tell you what to change.

### Guaranteed Outcomes
We provide findings and recommendations based on our analysis. We cannot guarantee that implementing our recommendations will produce specific business outcomes. Results depend on execution, context, and change over time.

## Data Handling

- Intake data is used only for the purpose of conducting the audit
- We do not share your data with third parties
- If you provide a repo URL, we request read-only access (or a zip)
- We do not store your passwords, API keys, or credentials
- After delivery and the follow-up window, order data is archived
- You can request deletion of your data at any time

## Refund Policy

- If we have not started the audit: full refund
- If the audit has started: we will work with you to make the deliverable useful
- If the deliverable does not match the package description: refund or redo
- Refund requests should be made within 14 days of delivery

## Manual Owner Boundaries

The following actions ALWAYS require the human owner:
- Account creation (Stripe, email provider, Vercel, etc.)
- Payment/financial decisions
- Quality sign-off on delivered audits
- Deletion of order data
- Changes to pricing or service offerings
- Any action with legal or financial implications
